free educational apps on google play

Play free kids (a)cademy apps from Google Play Store. Click app icon for details or open app on store.

  • Animals Atlas

    Interesting facts with images and sounds of animals from all over the world, both domesticated and wild.

  • Birds Atlas

    Interesting facts with images and sounds of birds from all over the world, both domesticated and wild.

  • Car Brands Quiz

    Car Brands challenge you to recognize the logo of car manufacturers from all over the world, active or ceased.

  • Fables

    Fables for kids, small and big alike. Fables are part of human culture for 2000+ years and images are from Milo Winter book.

  • Learn Colors

    Learn colors by name and associate with objects. Learn Colors combines images and voices for fun and easy colors learning.

  • Musical Instruments Atlas

    Musical instruments from all over the world, some of which you never heard about. Music is part of human culture defining our evolution throughout history. Musical Instruments Atlas shows a glimpse on the variety of musical instruments used around the world.

  • Sounds Quiz

    Explore Sounds Quiz and learn by playing. Sounds Quiz uses images and relevant sound samples to help associate sounds with images and names.

  • Musical Instruments Quiz

    Musical Instruments Quiz is a trivia game that challenges you to recognize musical instruments existing all over the world, from well know guitar or piano to esoteric didgeridoo.
