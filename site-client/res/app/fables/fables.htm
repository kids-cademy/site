100 English fables, usually attributed to Aesop, with pictures and audio. Collection is loaded on user device so that Fables can be used off-line. Features fables list and catalog for quick browsing and search by predefined criteria.

Fables are part of human culture for 2000+ years and images are from Milo Winter book published in 1919, considered to be in the public domain.
